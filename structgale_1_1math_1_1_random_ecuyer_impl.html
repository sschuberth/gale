Detailed Description

A random number generator based on the maximally equidistributed combined Tausworthe generator by L'Ecuyer.

The period of the generator is 2^88 (about 10^26) and it uses only three 32-bit integers to store its state, see http://root.cern.ch/root/html/TRandom2.html.
