Creates an orthographic projection matrix.

The intersection points of clip_left / clip_bottom and clip_right / clip_top on the near clipping plane are mapped to the lower left and upper right viewport corners respectively.

inlinestatic

Creates a generic projection matrix onto the plane given by normal n as seen from viewpoint v.

If p is true, the projection is perspective, else it is orthographic. For details, see http://www.cs.iastate.edu/~cs577/handouts/projection.pdf
