Returns a torus knot with knot radius rk and tube radius rt.

The tube consist of sk and st segments along the knot and tube respectively. w and h specify the width and height of the knot's spiral, which is obtained by looping through the hole p times with q revolutions before joining its ends, where p and q have to be relatively prime.

Generates a mesh's surface by calculating eval at every point on the grid of size s_segs by t_segs which is defined by walking from s_min to s_max in x-direction and from t_min to t_max in y-direction.

s_closed and t_closed denote whether start and end vertices should be shared to close the mesh in the respective direction.
